Lyravi is the intersection of intention and intelligence — designed to help you build habits that matter, track progress that inspires, and live with clarity every single day.

The story behind Lyravi

We created Lyravi because the apps we were using felt fragmented. Your calendar lived here, your habits there, your journal somewhere else. Your life isn't compartmentalized — so why should the tools that support it be?

We wanted something that brought together everything that matters: your daily priorities, your habits and streaks, your calendar, your reflections. And we wanted it to feel human — warm, thoughtful, never pushy. Something that understood that progress isn't about perfection. It's about rhythm.

That's Lyravi. One app. One rhythm. One life.

How Lyravi works

Every morning, Lyravi meets you with an AI Daily Brief — a personalized summary of your priorities, habits, and the day ahead. It learns what matters to you and surfaces it first.

Throughout the day, you track habits you're building, capture to-dos as they come up, and stay connected to your calendar. The app never lectures. It gently guides.

At day's end, reflect with thoughtful journal prompts. Over time, you'll see patterns in your mood, productivity, and growth — visualized in ways that inspire, not overwhelm.

Lyravi is the friend in your pocket who knows you're trying to do good work, build better habits, and live with intention.
